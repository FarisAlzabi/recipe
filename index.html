Cinnamon Toast

First you slice up some bread and get it ready.↘️

Then put the bread in the toaster for 30-40 until it looks like this ↘️

After the bread is a little brown put butter on it so it can melt. ↘️

the butter should be melted after it is put some cinnamon and sugar on top ↘️

Finaly put it in toaster again for a few seconds then u got breakfast ready. ↘️
